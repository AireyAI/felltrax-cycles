Whinlatter Forest

England's only true mountain forest. Purpose-built trails through stunning Lakeland scenery with technical rock sections, fast berms, and flowing singletrack. The Altura Trail is the main red-graded loop — expect roots, rocks, and big grins.

Grizedale Forest

Deep in the heart of the Lake District between Windermere and Coniston. Mixed blue and red graded trails through ancient woodland. North Face Trail offers technical challenges while the easier routes suit riders building confidence.

380m elevation

Skiddaw

Raw, natural, unforgiving. Skiddaw's backcountry lines reward strong riders with some of the most dramatic descents in the Lakes. Loose slate, exposed ridgelines, and views that make the lung-busting climb worth every pedal stroke.
